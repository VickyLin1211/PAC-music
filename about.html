PAC is a global platform designed to connect serious music students with world-class teachers across borders.

What is PAC

PAC is built to remove geographical limitations in music education. It creates access to high-level training, international perspectives, and real opportunities for students anywhere in the world.

By combining education, mentorship, and industry exposure, PAC redefines how musicians learn, grow, and connect globally.

Our Mission

We aim to make music education more accessible, global, and impactful.

PAC empowers students to learn from the best teachers worldwide while building meaningful pathways into the music industry.

Founder

PAC was founded by a Berklee College of Music student with a vision to redefine access in global music education.

Having experienced both opportunity and limitation firsthand, she recognized a global gap — where talent exists, but access does not.

PAC is built to bridge that gap, creating a platform where serious musicians can learn, connect, and grow without borders.
